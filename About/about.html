Our Story

From a Small Studio to a Global Tech Partner

Founded in 2016 in Hyderabad, Techechos Innovation started with a simple belief: technology should empower every business, not just the Fortune 500. What began as a three-person web studio has grown into a full-service technology company trusted by clients across India, the Middle East, and beyond.

Today, we are a team of 120+ specialists — software engineers, AI researchers, cloud architects, and product designers — united by an obsession with craft, quality, and client outcomes.

We've delivered 200+ projects, built products that have reached millions of users, and helped startups scale from seed to Series B.

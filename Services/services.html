How do I get started with a project?

▾

Simply reach out via our contact form or book a free consultation. We'll start with a discovery call to understand your needs, followed by a scoping document and a formal proposal.

What technologies do you specialize in?

▾

We work across the modern tech stack including React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, Flutter, and AWS/GCP for cloud infrastructure. We always choose the right tool for your specific requirements.

Do you provide post-launch support?

▾

Yes. Every project comes with a standard 30-day warranty. After that, we offer dedicated technical support and maintenance SLA retainers to ensure your system remains healthy.

How long does a typical project take?

▾

A simple website or MVP can take 4–6 weeks. Complex enterprise applications or mobile apps typically range from 3 to 6 months depending on the scope and integrations required.
